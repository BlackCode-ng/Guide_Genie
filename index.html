Welcome to GuideGenie where we provide personalised university guidance for high school students.

Our team of experienced consultants is dedicated to helping you achieve your educational goals and unlocking your full potential.

Our mission is to make university selection process easier and more accessible for sudents and their family. We understand that choosing a university and course can be overwhelming, which is why we offer tailored advising sessions to guide you towards your dream university and career path. We are committed to excellence and maintain the highest standards of professionalism and integrity in all our interactions. Our consultants are experts in their field and have a deep understanding of university application process. We pride ourselves on providing accurate and up-to-date information on universities and courses.

At GuideGenie, we believe in personalized attention and guidance.

Our consulting sessions are designed to be interactive and collaborative, with the goal of empowering you to make informed decisions about your future. We take the time to get to know you, your interests, and your aspirations, so that we can provide individualized recommendations and support. Our commitment to our clients doesn't end with consulting sessions. We provide ongoing support and assistance throughout the application process, from essay writing to interview preperation. We are here to ensure that you have the best chance of success.
